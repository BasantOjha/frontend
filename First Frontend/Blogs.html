Internet Technology


Internet is a Global System of interconnected computer networks also called as network of network. It was conceived by Advanced Research Project Agency (ARPA) in 1969 and was called as ARPANet. Some of the features of the internet are as follows:

  • Online Shopping

  • Sending and receiving E-mails

  • For promotion of business and inovation

  • Chatting with others

  • Playing online games

  • Socializing with others



The most important use of internet is that we can get information and education from it. It provides us with various sites and blogs that give us informative contents that helps us in studies. It helps people to learn various things and help them to get knowledge which they implement in their daily life. It has left a huge impact in pur lives.In earlier days we used to go to the libraries to search information about something but now we are able to get that information in just few clicks. The internet has provided us with the useful knowledge and information that is very useful for social, personal and economic development.
